## 10. White House Executive Order: Promoting Advanced Artificial Intelligence Innovation and Security
- 출처: https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2026/06/promoting-advanced-artificial-intelligence-innovation-and-security/
- 발행: 2026-06-07 (The White House)
- 본문:
  The White House issued an executive order promoting advanced artificial intelligence innovation and security, establishing federal-level policy to accelerate AI development while safeguarding national security.
  The order directs federal agencies to prioritize AI adoption, reduce regulatory barriers for AI developers, and expand access to government data for AI training purposes.
  It establishes a new interagency task force to coordinate AI security standards across critical infrastructure sectors, including energy, finance, healthcare, and transportation.
  The order also calls for increased investment in AI research and development, with a focus on maintaining U.S. competitiveness against international rivals in the AI race.
  Additionally, it directs the Department of Commerce to update export controls on advanced AI chips and models to prevent adversaries from accessing cutting-edge AI capabilities.

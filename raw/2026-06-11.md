## 1. SKT·NTT·청화통신, 5억 달러 규모 'IOWN AI 펀드' 공동 설립
- 출처: https://www.koreatimes.co.kr/business/companies/20260610/skt-ntt-chunghwa-telecom-to-jointly-launch-500-mil-ai-fund
- 발행: 2026-06-10
- 본문:
  SK Telecom has announced it will establish a $500 million artificial intelligence (AI) investment fund with Japan's NTT and Taiwan's Chunghwa Telecom, marking a cross-border push by three of East Asia's largest telecommunications companies to jointly develop the global AI ecosystem.
  The fund, called the IOWN AI Fund — named after NTT's Innovative Optical and Wireless Network initiative — will be managed through a newly incorporated fund management company called Catalight Capital, jointly established by the three telecom companies and based across Silicon Valley and East Asia.
  The fund will invest in companies developing technologies for AI data centers, including power-efficiency solutions and liquid cooling, as well as AI chips such as accelerators, graphics processing units and neural processing units.
  It will also target distributed cloud systems, optical communications, AI software for inference optimization and AI applications in the health care, manufacturing and finance sectors.
  NTT said roughly 20 major global corporations, including Sony and Toshiba, have expressed interest in joining the fund as limited partners. SK hynix is also reportedly preparing to participate.

## 5. Microsoft Build 2026 — MAI-Code-1-Flash·MAI-Thinking-1 자체 AI 모델 공개
- 출처: https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/02/microsoft-unveils-new-ai-models-lessen-reliance-on-openai-lower-costs.html
- 발행: 2026-06-02
- 본문:
  Microsoft announced MAI-Code-1-Flash, its inaugural model that takes written descriptions from people and spits out source code for applications and websites, making a concerted effort to compete with proprietary models.
  MAI-Code-1-Flash is Microsoft's first coding model built entirely without OpenAI, with a sparse Mixture-of-Experts architecture featuring 137 billion total parameters, a 256,000-token context window, and training through roughly May 2026.
  It was trained from the ground up on clean, traceable and enterprise-grade data, without distillation from third-party models, trained on the exact agentic harnesses developers use every day.
  It outperforms Claude Haiku 4.5 with better price to performance across coding benchmarks. The model is rolling out to GitHub Copilot individual users in Visual Studio Code.
  At Build 2026, Microsoft also unveiled MAI-Thinking-1, its first fully in-house reasoning model, alongside MAI-Voice-2, MAI-Image-2.5, and MAI-Transcribe-1.5.

## 9. SoftBank·Sony·Honda·NEC, 1조 파라미터 물리 AI 모델 개발 합작사 설립
- 출처: https://techwireasia.com/2026/04/japan-physical-ai-softbank-nec-honda-sony/
- 발행: 2026-04-13
- 본문:
  SoftBank Corp., NEC Corp., Sony Group Corp., and Honda Motor Co. publicly unveiled a new joint venture — internally branded Japan AI Foundation Model Development — designed to build a domestically produced, trillion-parameter foundation model focused on physical AI: the class of systems that power robots, factory lines, and autonomous vehicles rather than chatbots.
  Up to ¥1 trillion (roughly $6.3 billion) of NEDO funding will flow into the program over five years beginning fiscal 2026, with each of the four founders holding equity stakes above 10%.
  Honda is contributing more than 2.3 million hours of teleoperated driving data plus ASIMO-lineage manipulation traces. Sony is granting access to image-sensor labeled datasets covering the majority of the world's industrial CMOS workloads.
  Japan AI Foundation Model aims to develop an ultra-large model with 1 trillion parameters by 2027, with the target to introduce AI into practical applications by 2030.
  A public-private joint venture has drawn investment interest from about 30 companies, including chemicals and materials firms Asahi Kasei and Yaskawa Electric, Fujitsu, and major transportation firms.

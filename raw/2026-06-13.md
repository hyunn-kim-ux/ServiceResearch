## 9. SoftBank, NEC, Honda, Sony Launch Japan AI Foundation Model Development Company
- 출처: https://techwireasia.com/2026/04/japan-physical-ai-softbank-nec-honda-sony/
- 발행: 2026-04-13
- 본문:
  SoftBank, Sony, Honda, and NEC officially launched a joint venture — Japan AI Foundation Model Development — with a singular mission: to build large-scale AI foundation models entirely on Japanese soil, trained on Japanese data, deployed in Japanese factories, hospitals, and cars.
  The joint venture is designed to build a domestically produced, trillion-parameter foundation model focused on physical AI: the class of systems that power robots, factory lines, and autonomous vehicles.
  Up to ¥1 trillion (roughly $6.3 billion) of New Energy and Industrial Technology Development Organization (NEDO) funding will flow into the program over five years beginning fiscal 2026.
  SoftBank and NEC will lead AI foundation model development, while Sony and Honda will deploy the developed AI across automotive, robotics, gaming, and semiconductor sectors.
  Nippon Steel, Kobe Steel, and Japan's three largest megabanks all took equity stakes.
